Welcome to the **Klaxo Journal** — the home for our latest travel stories, driving
guides and rental tips from Munich and across Bavaria.

## Written in the dashboard, published in minutes

Every article here is written in the Klaxo admin dashboard using a simple visual
editor. You add a headline, a cover image, write the story, and hit **Publish** —
the site rebuilds itself automatically and the post appears on this page. No code,
no copy-pasting HTML.

### What you can add to a post

- A **headline** and a short **summary**
- A **category** badge (News, Day Trips, Alpine Transfers, and so on)
- A **cover image** for the hero and the article card
- The **story** itself, with headings, lists, links and images

> Tip: keep paragraphs short and lead with the most useful information — travellers
> skim before they read.
